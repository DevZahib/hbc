Chapter One
The Legend of the
Hairless Butt Cheek
Once upon a time, in a forest so wide, lived a Butt Cheek so hairless — with nothing to hide. He wore green underwear, every day, every night, and his powers were TERRIBLE — a truly wild sight.

If he pooped, five enemies would perish on the spot. If he farted, three more would drop. The Hairless Butt Cheek wore this power lightly — and always kept a pot of super glue close by, just in case.

One day in the forest he strolled along the path, when a dinosaur pounced and let out a laugh: "I'll chomp off your cheek! Ha ha ha HA!" And it did — with a CRUNCH — leaving one great big scar!

The Hairless Butt Cheek looked down. One cheek: gone. The dinosaur grinned. A terrible grin. So HBC did what any reasonable butt would do — he pooped directly on the dinosaur's face. The dinosaur died immediately.

He grabbed the glue pot, found his missing cheek, and stuck it right back on. He walked off into the sunset, green underwear gleaming. The end... of Part One.

⭐Fun Fact: All Hairless Butt Cheeks wear underwear at all times. Even sleeping. Even in the shower. This is the law.
Chapter Two
The Mark
One week went by and the butt thought, "What fun!" "I'll go for a swim in the ocean and sun!" But his cheek was still borrowed — held on by old tape, and a shark smelled the tape and went for a scrape.

CHOMP! The shark ate the tape clean off. The cheek stayed on — but it left a mark. A red ring. A stamp. A very embarrassing blemish that all other Hairless Butt Cheeks would surely notice and mock forever.

He swam to the shore and went straight to the doc, Dr. Fatzmann, who stood there in his medical smock. "I've a mark!" cried the Butt Cheek, all shaky and pale. "Fat Spray!" cried the doctor. "It never can fail!"

The spray was applied. The mark was covered. And then... it grew. And grew. And GREW. The Hairless Butt Cheek inflated like a very unfortunate balloon.

Then — BOOM. He exploded. And out of the explosion tumbled fifteen tiny, wriggling Baby Hairless Butt Cheeks, each in their own tiny green underwear.

Chapter Three
The Hairless Butt Cheek Babies
Fifteen babies popped out of the smoky big blast, each with green underwear, going quite fast. But one had a mark! And another let a fart — and the marked baby died. Fourteen made a fresh start.

The fourteen babies wandered out onto the golden prairie, blinking in the sunshine. They had never seen a prairie before. They had only just been born, after all — roughly forty-five seconds ago.

A cheetah was chasing a gazelle cross the land, and the babies knew just what to do — they had a plan! They launched a great POOP STORM at the gazelle's rear, and the gazelle dropped down — and the cheetah gave a cheer.

The Cheetah skidded to a halt, stared at the babies, and declared them to be the most impressive creatures it had ever seen. From that moment on, the Cheetah was their BFF — Best Friend Forever.

⭐Fun Fact: All Hairless Butt Cheeks wear underwear. The babies each came with their own pair, pre-fitted, right out of the explosion. Nobody knows how.
Chapter Four
The Ghost
The babies, the Cheetah — they fell in a hole! The Cheetah dug upward, brave heart and brave soul. But a lion was waiting at the top of the gap, and swallowed them all in one terrible snap.

Nobody had noticed something important: when the original Hairless Butt Cheek exploded at the doctor's office, he had left behind an evil ghost. The ghost floated through the night, cackling, planning terrible revenge on everyone.

The ghost found the Witch Doctor's rickety shack, and burst through the door with a mighty crack! But the doctor was testing his sprays — NICE and HBC — and a patient kept fanning them right back at thee.

The sprays hit the ghost. Both at once. The ghost stopped cackling. Its mark vanished. Its underwear turned a brilliant, vivid green. The Hairless Butt Cheek was himself again — alive, happy, and extremely well-dressed.

He went to the zoo to celebrate. A giraffe looked at his green underwear. The giraffe thought it was a leaf. The giraffe yanked. HBC pooped directly in the giraffe's mouth. The giraffe died. He got the underwear back.

Chapter Five
The Car
He wanted a car. So he bought one that night. But he sat in the seat and turned green with fright. He tried once — and pooped! The car stalled with a groan. So he walked to the dealership. Quite alone.

"Your cars are bad quality!" said the Hairless Butt Cheek. "No they are NOT," said the manager. "You cannot talk back to me," said HBC. The manager did not listen. This was a mistake.

He pooped in the manager's butt hole right there. The manager died. It was perfectly fair. Then he stole the manager's car — far superior indeed — and drove it straight into the ocean at speed.

SPLASH. The car sank gently to the ocean floor and settled into the sand. The Hairless Butt Cheek looked around. It was quiet down there. Peaceful. The fish were pleasant company. This was home now.

The only problem: no furniture. And no money to buy any.

Chapter Six
The Pig
Now Piglwiglskunkl was a pig of great fame, a friend of the Butt Cheek — quite wild, quite tame. He found a strange wig on the grass in the park, put it on — and a portal appeared in the dark!

He stepped through and fell asleep. When he woke up, he was inside a toilet bowl. A toy toilet bowl. He had been shrunk down. And the toilet — was Charlie. His sworn enemy.

Charlie had a pet owl named Evil Eye, whose entire job was to peck Piglwiglskunkl. Things looked grim.

But along came big Benny, a bear with a feast — "It's Thanksgiving!" he said. "And I want that owl beast!" He ate Evil Eye. Then he secretly placed some nerd glasses on Charlie — right on his face.

When Charlie noticed the glasses, he was so enraged that he exploded. Piglwiglskunkl threw a party. Everyone came. Benny, however, forgot his underwear on the way out.

Chapter Seven
The Pigless Underwear
After the party the guests all went home, and Piglwiglskunkl was left there alone. He found some stray underwear — Benny's no doubt! He put them right on and he strutted about.

Then the underwear vanished. He found Benny wearing them. "Hand them over!" said Pig. "No," said Bear. A fight broke out. The Hairless Butt Cheek pooped on both of them. They both died. The underwear lay unclaimed on the floor.

HBC went to the pet store and bought a sheep named Shinshong Sheep, who was certified 100% bomb-proof. The same could not be said for his car.

Someone had planted a bomb on his car! He hadn't yet noticed. Walked up from afar. But a bee flew inside — and he stopped, turned away. KABOOM! The car blew. He was safe! What a day!

He ran back to the bee and thanked it sincerely. The bee said nothing. It was a bee. But it buzzed in a way that felt very warm.

Chapter Eight
The Flying Pig
He needed some furniture. He had no cash. He thought for a while, then came up with a dash of genius: rocket-booster shoes for a pig! Transport of the future! Really quite big!

Nobody bought them. He was furious. So he took the rocket booster shoes, flipped them upside down, attached them to a pig, flew it into the sky, and pressed the button. The pig shot downward.

KABLOOSH!!! The pig hit the Earth with a crack! It sounded like thunder! The sky folded back! And then — oh what wonder — it started to rain... not water, not hailstones, but BACON. Insane.

It was the best day ever. The pig crashed through the roof of a couch store and all the furniture flew directly into the Hairless Butt Cheek's underwater car home below.

The couch store owner gave everything away for free. He had no choice. A pig had just come through the ceiling.

🥓Meanwhile: A duck nearby smelled the bacon rain. The duck was allergic to bacon. The duck ate the bacon anyway.
Chapter Nine
The Brainless Duck
The duck ate the bacon. The bacon was wrong. Something went POP where his brain had been strong. His brain tumbled out. He felt terribly free. "I don't know who I am!" he said. "But a hero? That's me!"

The brainless duck saw a mean man bullying a small child on the street. He squared up. He ran at the man. He punched.

The man punched back. The duck went flying — straight into a tree. He bounced off the tree like a rubber ball and hit the man directly in the face with tremendous momentum.

The man flew and skidded and came to a stop. From now and forever — to the very last drop — his name would be different, no longer the same: Antmucky Fat Soup was his permanent name.

The kid was impressed. The duck was confused. And Antmucky Fat Soup had a very sore face.

Chapter Ten
Antmucky Fat Soup
Antmucky Fat Soup went to visit the doc, Doc Antysimphaleana — the best on the block. "Smart Juice!" said the doctor. "A spray on your head! You'll be clever again!" — but the brain grew instead.

The brain got bigger. And bigger. And bigger. Until — POP. Out of the dust came a Brain Monster who knew everything in the world, except karate.

He tried karate anyway. He saw a man disrespecting a kid. He punched the man. The man punched back. They flew around the world punching each other. It went on for a very long time.

The kid stopped them both with a wave of one hand, and they slammed to the ground — and it shook all the land! The impact was so mighty, so terribly vast, the Earth cracked in two. The split happened fast.

The Earth was now in two halves. This was considered a problem.

Chapter Eleven
The Dog Race
An alien appeared with a deal neat and bright: "I have enormous tape to fix the Earth right! But first beat me racing in one dogsled run — from start to the finish — whoever has won!"

Lickaleajanickaradoesta stepped up. The race began neck and neck. Then the alien pulled ahead — five miles, six, seven. It looked hopeless. Then the dogs went so fast they started to fly.

The dog flew too fast — and the poor creature died! But its body kept sliding, still moving with pride! It slid past the alien, it slid past the line — Lickaleajanickaradoesta won! Everything fine!

The alien handed over the enormous tape. Reluctantly. It was, as it turned out, a bomb.

Chapter Twelve
The Tape
They laid the great tape right across the Earth's crack, smoothing it carefully, front side and back. But then came a ticking — a tick and a tock — a countdown, a beeping, a very bad clock.

The tape was a bomb. Ten seconds after it was applied, it exploded. Half of the Earth disappeared. The Hairless Butt Cheek had been working on the tape at the time. Now he was half a butt. A full butt has sixty brains. Half a butt has only thirty. One loose brain flew off into the atmosphere.

The brain landed square in Antmucky's big head! He was smart! He was clever! But something wrong said: "I AM the Hairless Butt Cheek!" — and so he began to poop upon everyone. That was his plan.

He pooped on the heads of a bride and groom at a wedding. They called 911. Antmucky Fat Soup went to jail.

Chapter Thirteen
In the Jail Cell
He sat in the jail cell and thought and he planned, till he spotted a fire extinguisher at hand. "I'll sit on the handle! I'll BLAST through the bars!" He squeezed — and shot upward — past buildings and cars!

He flew all the way to California, where a huge forest fire was blazing. The extinguisher, still spraying, put out every last flame. The trees were saved. The animals cheered. A bear waved a tiny flag.

Then out of a boulder he carved with great care a statue of Butt Cheek — majestic and rare! The people all gathered and stared in delight: "This hero is ours! What a glorious sight!"

Antmucky Fat Soup became the President of Butt Cheek World. He wore the crown. He sat in the big chair. He looked very important.

He immediately started making really bad choices.

And so ends our tale — of the Butt, and the Mark, of sharks and of ghosts and of things in the dark, of bacon that rained and of tape that went BOOM — and a President ruling from his Butt Cheek room.
👑THE END. Antmucky Fat Soup is now President of Butt Cheek World and making really bad choices. The story may not truly be over.
